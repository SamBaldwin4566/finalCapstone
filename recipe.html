Jerk Jackfruit
ü§ç

Ingredients

  • Corn Tortillas
  • Jackfruit
  • Red Onion
  • Tomato
  • Coriander
  • Jerk Seasoning
  • Soy sauce
  • Limes

Recipe

  1. Squeeze the jackfruit in a tea towel to remove excess water.
  2. Marinate the jackfruit in soy sauce, jerk seasoning and lime juice for 1 hour.
  3. Fry the jackfruit in a tsp of oil, add a splash of water if it starts to look too dry.
  4. Make the pico de gallo by chopping the red onion, tomatoes and coriander and mixing in a bowl
  5. Prepare your taco in the tortilla, garnish with a lime wedge and ENJOY!

  • Chickpea Flour

Recipe

  1. Prepare the batter by mixing the flour with water until it forms an egg like consistency.
  2. Chop the tofu into small strips and wrap the nori around them.
  3. Dip the tofu pieces in the batter and try not to overcrowd the pan.
  4. Prepare the lettuce avocado and mango salsa.
  5. Put all the ingredients in a corn tortilla and ENJOY!
